About Fruits of Love Family

Fruits of Love Family is a passionate team of over 75 members, founded in Rwanda 🇷🇼. Our journey began in high school at CJSM (Centre de Jeunes Sourds Muets) in Huye District, Ngoma Sector — also known as Saint Gabriel School. We started small, with less than 10 members, and a big heart focused on helping deaf children from very poor families who couldn't afford basic school materials or take care of themselves.

Our Mission

We aim to contribute to the social development of Rwanda and protect human life, especially by ensuring the good health and future of children. We help poor mothers in hospitals, support street children, and assist families living in extreme poverty.

Our Vision

Our dream is to see all street children off the streets — safe, in homes, and in school. We want to build a better future for the next generation, free from drugs and alcohol.

Our Activities

Helping Poor Children

We support children under 18 from poor families by providing food, clothes, and hygiene items.

Supporting Mothers in Hospitals

We visit hospitals to help struggling mothers with food, baby items, and sometimes hospital bills.

Finding Homes for Street Kids

We rent homes for kids on the street and help them settle into a safe environment.

Helping with School Materials

We provide school materials like books, pens, and uniforms for poor students.

Connecting People to Jobs

We help unemployed parents and older youth find jobs to support their families.

Community Support Programs

We organize community programs to support vulnerable families and promote social cohesion.
